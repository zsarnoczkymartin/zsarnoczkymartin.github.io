Abstract (English): This comprehensive research examines the quality of life and well-being of elderly residents in Nagykanizsa, Hungary, over a decade-long period (2009-2019). Based on survey data from 1,000 participants and in-depth interviews with stakeholders, the study reveals the "Nagykanizsa Model" - a unique community-based approach to active ageing through senior clubs and social engagement programs. Key findings demonstrate that elderly individuals participating in community activities report significantly higher life satisfaction and well-being scores compared to isolated peers.
